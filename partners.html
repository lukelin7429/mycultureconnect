Working together

Our partners

We are proud to collaborate with a diverse range of schools and organizations to promote language learning, cultural exchange, and educational excellence.

In Taiwan

100+ public schools across central Taiwan

From the coast of Changhua to the hills of Nantou, we work hand-in-hand with local schools to bring bilingual learning and global connection to students who need it most.

Our partnerships make free English practice, school websites, and international exchange possible for thousands of children each year.

Featured partnership

CCC Chinese School

Since 2022, our partnership with CCC Chinese School in Albany, New York has grown into a two-way exchange.

Students from CCC tutor our elementary children in English, and in July 2024, fourteen CCC students travelled to Taiwan for volunteer work. The partnership continued through 2025 with further educational and cultural exchanges.
