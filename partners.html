Working together

Our partners

We are proud to collaborate with a diverse range of schools and organizations to promote language learning, cultural exchange, and educational excellence.

In Taiwan

100+ public schools across central Taiwan

From the coast of Changhua to the hills of Nantou, we work hand-in-hand with local schools to bring bilingual learning and global connection to students who need it most.

One of our most demanding undertakings is building English–Chinese bilingual websites for Taiwan's schools — giving their students, teachers and stories a place on the world stage. Every site is researched, written and hand-coded school by school. It is slow, painstaking work, created and maintained entirely by us, so that even the smallest rural school can connect with the wider world.
