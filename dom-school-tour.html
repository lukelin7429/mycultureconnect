Dom Jones in Changhua classrooms

A school assembly tour built around English education, global connections, and kindness.

From Compton to Changhua

Real English, global stories, and a message every student can carry forward.

Dom Jones is an internationally recognized social advocate, educator, and media personality from California. Since December 2025, she has partnered with My Culture Connect to bring a school assembly tour to Changhua County.

Each campus visit gives students the chance to hear living English from an American speaker, connect their local school to the wider world, and reflect on kindness, courage, and the value of their own voice.

"Be the love you did not receive."

Three commitments

What Dom brings to every assembly

01

English Education

Students hear natural, lived English from a working American media personality and respond in a real assembly setting.

02

Global Connections

Dom connects Changhua classrooms with Los Angeles, the United Nations, and the wider world students are preparing to enter.

03

Kindness

Every visit centers empathy, unity, and the belief that every student's voice and dreams matter.
