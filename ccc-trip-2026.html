CCC School · Summer 2026 in Changhua

A small team of CCC student volunteers returns to Changhua this August — teaching English in the mornings and discovering Taiwanese culture in the afternoons. This page grows with the plan, and will hold our highlights once the week is done.

The week ahead

One week, two halves of every day

From Monday, August 3 to Friday, August 7, 2026, the volunteers spend their mornings — 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 noon — as English-teaching helpers at Zhonghe Elementary School in Pitou Township, Changhua. They work with 20 local pupils entering grades 5 and 6, divided into two groups. Each afternoon is a cultural experience across the county — the details are still being arranged and will appear here as they are confirmed.

Getting here

Airport → High Speed Rail → Changhua

From Taoyuan International Airport, the journey to Changhua HSR Station takes around 75–90 minutes total and requires two legs: the Airport MRT to HSR Taoyuan Station, then the High Speed Rail south to Changhua.

从桃园国际机场到彰化高铁站全程约75–90分钟，分两段：先搭机场捷运至高铁桃园站，再转乘台湾高铁南下至彰化站。

1

Arrive at Taoyuan International Airport

抵达桃园国际机场

Both Terminal 1 and Terminal 2 have direct access to the Airport MRT (桃园机场捷运). Follow signs for MRT / 捷运 after clearing customs and collecting luggage.

过海关并取完行李后，跟随MRT / 捷运指示牌前往机场捷运月台。第一航厦（T1）与第二航厦（T2）均有直接进站通道。

✈ Taoyuan International Airport · 桃园国际机场
2

Take the Airport MRT to HSR Taoyuan Station

搭乘机场捷运至高铁桃园站

Board the Airport MRT Direct Train (直达车, purple express) — it stops at HSR Taoyuan Station (A18) in about 17 minutes from Terminal 2. Commuter trains (普通车) also work but take longer (~35 min).

搭乘直达车（紫色快车）约 17分钟 可达 高铁桃园站（A18站）。普通车也可搭，但需时约35分钟，停靠站较多。

💡 Buy a single-journey ticket or use an EasyCard (悠游卡). Fare from T2 to HSR Taoyuan is approx. NT$35.
可购单程票或使用悠游卡（EasyCard）刷卡进站，T2至高铁桃园站票价约新台币35元。
🚇 Airport MRT · 机场捷运 · ~17 min
3

Board the Taiwan High Speed Rail — southbound to Changhua

转乘台湾高铁，南下至彰化站

Exit the MRT and follow signs to the HSR (高鐵) ticketing hall. Purchase a ticket for Changhua Station (彰化站). Take any southbound train — standard and business class are both fine. The journey takes roughly 50–60 minutes.

出捷运站后跟随高铁指标进入售票大厅，购买前往彰化站的车票，搭乘任何南下班次即可（标准车厢或商务车厢均可）。车程约 50–60分钟。

💡 Seats can be reserved online at thsrc.com.tw or at the station kiosks. Trains run frequently — roughly every 15–30 min during peak hours.
可至 thsrc.com.tw 官网提前订座，或在车站自动售票机购票。高峰时段约每15–30分钟一班。
🚄 Taiwan HSR · 台湾高铁 · ~55 min
4

Arrive at Changhua HSR Station — we will meet you here

抵达彰化高铁站，我们在这里接您

Changhua Station is located in Tianzhong Township. A My Culture Connect representative (or your host family) will be waiting for you at the exit / pick-up area. Please message us when your train departs Taoyuan so we know when to expect you.

彰化站位于田中镇。人师教育协会代表或接待家庭将在出站接送区等候您。请在高铁发车时传讯息给我们，方便安排接站时间。

📍 Changhua HSR Station · 彰化高铁站（田中镇）
