CCC School · Summer 2026 in Changhua

A small team of CCC student volunteers returns to Changhua this August — teaching English in the mornings and discovering Taiwanese culture in the afternoons. This page grows with the plan, and will hold our highlights once the week is done.

The week ahead

One week, two halves of every day

From Monday, August 3 to Friday, August 7, 2026, the volunteers spend their mornings as English-teaching helpers at Zhonghe Elementary School in Pitou Township, Changhua. Each afternoon is a cultural experience across the county — the details are still being arranged and will appear here as they are confirmed.
