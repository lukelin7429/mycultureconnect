UN Youth Climate Ambassador

Edward Huang

Member of UNFCCC YOUNGO, International Ambassador for LCOY Taiwan, and a UCLA graduate. Through My Culture Connect, Edward opens real international stages for Taiwan's students — from UN climate summits to SDG project learning.

Biography

From California to the UN, from Taiwan to the global youth stage

Edward Huang is based in Irvine, California and holds a Master's degree from UCLA. He is a member of YOUNGO, the official Children and Youth Constituency of the UNFCCC, and has been active in global youth climate action since 2023.

As International Ambassador for LCOY Taiwan and a youth coach, he guides students in connecting their own interests with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) — amplifying Taiwan's visibility and impact in global climate action through the voices of young people.
