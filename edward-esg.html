For Companies · ESG

ESG goals, met by the next generation

LCOY Taiwan connects companies' sustainability commitments with youth talent, green-job pipelines and a genuine UN climate platform — turning ESG reporting into real engagement.

🌱 Green Jobs 🌐 UN Climate Stage 🤝 USR × CSR

💼 Youth green jobs

Taiwan's companies need to pay more attention to sustainability and ESG-related fields. As we expand opportunities, we should also identify whether the real issue is "a shortage of talent" or "a shortage of openings" — so that academia and industry develop talent that aligns with real market needs.

🌐 ESG & international climate action (COY / COP)

Helping corporate sustainability (ESG) stakeholders build a foundational understanding of international climate conferences such as COY and COP. LCOY Taiwan enables Taiwanese companies and young people to connect with the global scope, and helps Taiwan engage in international climate governance and action frameworks.

Why companies partner

Three ways an ESG partnership pays off

01

A green-talent pipeline

Internship matching and industry-academia links connect your teams with university students already trained in SDGs, climate action and project-based work.

02

Credible ESG engagement

Support for a UNFCCC-recognized youth platform is concrete, reportable ESG action — backed by UNFCCC and UNESCO-associated recognition, not just a slogan.

03

International visibility

Youth projects and the LCOY Taiwan forum draw mainstream media coverage, raising your brand's sustainability profile at home and on the UN stage.

Why it's credible

International recognition, not just a logo on a banner

LCOY Taiwan is hosted by Youth Network under UNFCCC YOUNGO recognition, with an exclusive partnership with PAC (People Achievement Consulting) — a recognized partner of NCDA (National Career Development Association) and a holder of GRI (Global Reporting Initiative) recognition, strengthening the project's professionalism and credibility in sustainability reporting and ESG standards.

How to partner

Three levels of partnership

From brand visibility to co-building international influence — choose the depth that fits your ESG goals.

01 · Foundation

Brand visibility

Be seen across the platform.
  • Title sponsorship and full-event brand logo exposure
  • Official exposure across the LCOY Taiwan social network
  • Logo featured in official promotional materials
02 · Deep engagement

ESG action link

All Foundation benefits, plus real youth engagement.
  • Staff run their own UNFCCC LCOY Taiwan workshop after training
  • University students join company Open House exchanges; graduate internship matching
  • Co-created sustainability content for your annual report
03 · Strategic

International influence

All Deep benefits, raised to the global stage.
  • Brand exposure at UN climate forums such as COY / COP
  • Co-publish a youth climate-policy white paper
  • Deepened influence and representation in international sustainability issues
