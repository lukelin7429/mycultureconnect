Meet our team

Leadership

Founder & President

Founder

Luke Lin

In 2001, Luke became a full-time volunteer teacher in rural Taiwan. For over two decades he has offered free English classes and organized events promoting higher education and cultural exchange. He serves as a consultant to Changhua’s International Education and English Education Center, supporting 70+ foreign English teachers, sits on the board of the Chinese Culture Connection in Boston, and directs several educational projects. Under his leadership, My Culture Connect continues to connect classrooms across Taiwan with people worldwide.

President

Kevin Yu

Kevin served as president from 2014–2017 and returned for a second term in 2023. A dedicated volunteer since MCC’s founding in 2009, he has helped shape the organization’s vision through many milestones. His collaborative leadership has strengthened our volunteer networks and inspired new programs, guided by an unwavering belief in the power of education and cross-cultural understanding.
