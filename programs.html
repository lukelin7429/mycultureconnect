Resources · Dom Jones

From Compton to Changhua.

Dom Jones — internationally recognized social advocate, educator, and media personality from California — has chosen Taiwan as her home. Since December 2025, she has partnered with My Culture Connect to bring a nationwide school assembly tour to Changhua County, built around three pillars: English education, global connections, and kindness.

Born in the challenging streets of Compton to a mother of thirteen, Dom grew up in poverty and homelessness — and refused to be defined by it. She pursued higher education across the Midwest and Southern California in the humanities, communication studies, and women's studies.

With nearly 20 years in fitness, yoga, and wellness advocacy, she is also the owner and CEO of Propel Cycle, Southern California's premier indoor cycling studio. She hosts The Dom Jones Show on Pho Bolsa TV (PBTV), reaching millions of viewers across the United States and Vietnam.

In 2022, Dom appeared on CBS's The Amazing Race (Season 34), sharing her message of empathy and cross-cultural understanding with a global audience. As Government Affairs Ambassador for the United Nations Association — Orange County Chapter, she has dedicated her career to advancing the UN 2030 Agenda.

"Growing up without the love, safety, and guidance every child deserves, Dom made a vow to become the love she did not receive. Her life mission is to give kindness, unity, and hope to the world — especially to young people who need to know their voice and dreams matter." — Dom Jones

Our Mission

Every campus visit is built around three commitments that Dom carries from California to every Changhua classroom.

🗣️

English Education

Real, lived English in every classroom. Dom speaks to students as a working American media personality — every word is something she would actually say in California tomorrow, not textbook English.

🌐

Global Connections

From Compton to the UN to Taiwan. Dom shows students that Changhua is connected to Los Angeles, to Hanoi, to the UN — and that their voices matter on every one of those stages.

💛

Kindness

"Be the love you did not receive." Dom leads every assembly with empathy and unity, and the belief that every student's dreams matter — especially those who are told they don't.
