The Dom Jones School Tour

Chair of our Advocacy Board and UN SDG Ambassador, Dom Jones is travelling across Taiwan — sharing global citizenship, empathy, and a love of English with thousands of students.

Mission · Why is Dom in Taiwan?

From Compton to the world stage

Dom Jones is an internationally recognized social advocate, educator and media personality from California. She has chosen to make Taiwan her home, committed to serving its youth, schools and communities through cultural exchange and global education.

Since December 2025, Dom has partnered with My Culture Connect to launch a nationwide school assembly tour — walking into classrooms across Taiwan to share global citizenship, empathy, English motivation, and the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

Her story

An extraordinary journey, a gift to every student

Against the odds

Born on the challenging streets of Compton and raised by a mother of thirteen, Dom refused to be defined by poverty — pursuing higher education in the humanities, communication and women’s studies.

A global voice

With nearly 20 years in fitness and wellness advocacy, she even learned Vietnamese and Tagalog to amplify diverse communities. In 2022 she appeared on CBS’s The Amazing Race (Season 34).

Serving Taiwan

During the 2024 U.S. elections, over 100,000 citizens voted to have her represent them. Today she has chosen Taiwan as home — turning her journey into a gift for every student she meets.
