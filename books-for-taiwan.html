Books for Taiwan

Founded by Amy Lin, Books for Taiwan has placed thousands of English books into the hands of children across rural Changhua — and the schools wrote back to say thank you.

The gift of reading

Thousands of English books, given freely

Books for Taiwan collects and donates English-language books to schools that rarely have them. For children learning English far from any native speaker, a shelf of real books changes what is possible.
